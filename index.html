  • IoT Enabled Carrier Lift System for Retrieval and Delivery of Essentials
    Aayush Rai
    Bachelor's Major Thesis | Patent Filed (Publication No. 12/2025)
    A mechanical lift system designed for buildings with space constraints to carry weights up to 20kg across multiple floors. Features include: 3D-modeled structure using SolidWorks with maximum area requirement of 135 in², remote operation via Blynk IoT and Sinric Pro, solar-powered with closed-loop Battery Management System, and a movable joint with 2 DOF (±90°). The device assists elderly people, individuals with motor impairments, and delivery agents. Patent published on Indian Patent Office website.
  • Hand Gesture Operated Multipoint Control Switch
    Aayush Rai
    Research Paper Under Review | Clinical Trials in Progress
    Edge computing-based system using Raspberry Pi, ESP-32 CAM, OpenCV, MediaPipe, and Machine Learning to enable people with motor impairments to control their environment through hand gestures. Designed specifically for users with limited hand motions and finger angles. Research paper drafted in LaTeX covering caregiver stress reduction and benefits of autonomous edge-based systems. Clinical trials planned in controlled and hospital environments under guidance of Dr. RS Saxena (Scientist G, SSPL, DRDO) at Lok Nayak Jai Prakash Hospital.
